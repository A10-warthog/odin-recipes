Greek Lemon Chicken Soup

Description

A hearty bowlful soup made with most familiar ingredients from the mediterranean which will make up feel warm from the inside. This Greek Lemon chicken soup is a perfect introduction to a full Greek meal in itself. Serve with fresh pita triangles.

Avgolemono is a family of sauces and soups made with egg yolk and lemon juice mixed with broth, heated until they thicken. This dish is one of the many type of Avgolemonon.

Ingredients

  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped celery
  • 6 tablespoons chicken soup base
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground white pepper
  • 1/4 cup margarine
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 8 egg yolks
  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 1 cup diced, cooked chicken meat
  • 16 slices lemon

Steps:

  1. Combine chicken broth, lemon juice, carrots, onions, celery, soup base, and white pepper in a large pot. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Blend margarine and flour in a small bowl; gradually stir into soup mixture. Simmer, stirring frequently, for 10 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, beat egg yolks in a bowl until light in color. Gradually whisk in some hot soup. Add egg mixture to the pot and heat through.
  4. Add rice and chicken; cook until warmed through. Ladle hot soup into bowls and garnish with lemon slices.
